Fall 2016 Coding Workshop



FEMMES is a national organization dedicated to engaging middle school girls in STEM in an effort to increase gender parity. The UChicago chapter of FEMMES focuses specifically on bringing hands-on CS activities to young girls in the Hyde Park community.

This fall FEMMES will be holding an 8-week coding workshop to teach 7th and 8th grade girls Python programming skills Wednesdays after school at Kenwood Academy. The program will meet weekly on Wednesdays from 4:20pm to 5:50pm beginning October 5th and ending November 30th (Nov 23rd will be off in observance of Thanksgiving holidays).


Please email contact@femmes.io to sign up or with any questions.
