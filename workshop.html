2016 UChicago FEMMES Coding Workshop



A 10-week workshop for local girls in grades 7 & 8 who have expressed an interest in computer science. Fundamentals of computer science will be taught in depth. The curriculum covers variables, control flow, loops, arrays, and more through hands on activities and the creation of computer games in Python. Classes will begin in March. Check back soon to sign up! For now you can email femmes@uchicago.edu with questions or for more information.
