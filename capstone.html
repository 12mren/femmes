    • Capstone Event: UChicago FEMMES Code Camp

    Our Capstone Event is an annual event held in the spring on the University of Chicago campus for female students from middle schools across the south side of Chicago. The event will feature a keynote speaker and hands-on activities in computer science led by University of Chicago professors, graduate and undergraduate students, and special guests. The event runs all day from 10am-5pm. Lunch and snacks are provided. If you or your school is interested in attending our Capstone Event please fill out the following interest form. We will be letting schools know in early January if we have space to accommodate them at our 2015 event.
