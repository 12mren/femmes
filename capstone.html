    • Capstone Event

    Our Capstone Event is an annual event held in the spring on the University of Chicago campus for female students from middle schools across the south side of Chicago. The event will feature a keynote speaker and hands-on activities in computer science led by University of Chicago professors, graduate and undergraduate students, and special guests. The event runs all day from 9am-4pm. Lunch and breakfast are provided. If you or your school is interested in attending our Capstone Event please contact us here.
