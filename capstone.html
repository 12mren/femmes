2017 UChicago FEMMES Tech Capstone

April 22, 2017

UChicago FEMMES is now taking applications for its third annual Capstone event!

APPLY NOW


The FEMMES Capstone event encourages female middle school students to get involved with STEM fields. All students attending the Capstone event will get the opportunity to hear from speakers in STEM industries or academia and participate in hands-on workshops in subjects such as Biology, Computer Science, and Physics. Returning students from UChicago Code Camp 2016 will get the opportunity to attend STEM workshops designed and led by University of Chicago professors. First time students will participate in hands-on activities in Computer Science led by University of Chicago undergraduates on the fundamentals of computer programing. Registration for the event is currently open

Our First Code Camp

On February 28, 2015, FEMMES held its first UChicago FEMMES Code Camp. The Code Camp was a day-long event aimed to introduce girls from neighboring Chicago middle schools to computer science in a fun and interactive way.
